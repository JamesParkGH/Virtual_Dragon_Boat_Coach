VDBC Data and Privacy Policy

This privacy statement explains how VDBC collects, uses, disclose, and otherwise processes personal information with our VDBC platform.

Please contact us at email@email.ca if you have any questions after reading this document and viewing the corresponding links.

Data Security

The security, integrity, and confidentiality of your information are extremely important to us.

All video and processed data are encrypted. More details on data security measures can be found below:

  • Encrypt all stored user data using the Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) with key lengths of 128 bits (As recommended by Canadian Government)
  • Apply role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure only authorized personnel such as coach, administrators can access sensitive user data.



What personal information do we collect

Information about you:

  • Information you provide when recording a session (i.e., session URL, session name, etc.)
  • Your account information such as email address, user name
  • Device information, including IP address
  • Site usage data


How will we use this information

We may use your information to:

  • Deliver our dragon boat paddling technique analyzing services to you.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent activities that may violate our policies, pose safety issues, or be fraudulent or illegal.
  • Improve the VDBC technique analyzer and feedback provider algorithms.


Data storage, retention, and destruction

All data will be stored in the VDBC sql database using AES encryption with key lengths of 128 bits. The database will have maintenance every 6 months to reinforce storage data security. If a user wishes to have their data removed, they can delete their data from the VDBC dashboard. Otherwise, data will be retained for 2 years before it is automatically deleted by the VDBC system. Data that participants marked to be important would not be automatically deleted unless it is manually deleted by the user.



Who has access to the data

Role-based access control will be implemented. Normal users would have access to all of their data and non-sensitive data other users shared. Sensitive data is only accessible by the owner and authorized personnel such as VDBC administrators.



Privacy Policy Effective Time

This Privacy Statement is effective as of the date it is provided to you or April, 1st, 2025 whichever is earlier.
